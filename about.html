Poker @ CMU

The aim of this organization is to provide a proper learning environment for both enthusiasts and beginners alike to discuss and apply the strategies of all forms of poker, primarily Texas Hold'em.

We would like to teach our members the application of weighted outcomes and other poker concepts and in doing so, sharpen their analytical abilities and encourage them to strive for rationality both in and out of poker. We are open to all students at Carnegie Mellon University of all disciplines and backgrounds.
